Metadata

Below is a list of resources and documents we have created in the development of our metadata standard for DataSF:

  • Final Metadata Standard (gsheet). Our final approved metadata standard.
  • Project Document and Major Steps (gdoc). Project charter for creating the City's metadata standard.
  • Metadata: Existing Practices and Survey (gdoc). Research conducted by our intern on what metadata standards exist, how other localities are using them and recommendations for San Francisco.
  • Metadata: Analysis and Inventories of Metadata Standards (gsheet). Spreadsheet listing and comparing the fields from various metadata standards at the local, state and federal level.

Data Inventory Guidebooks

As part of our legislation, we are inventorying the City's datasets. The guidebooks below describe the process and guidance we are providing to our data coordinators.

  • Data Coordinator Guidebook. The full data coordinator guidebook is available as a google doc or as a PDF.
  • Data Coordinator Detailed Inventory Guide: Steps 2 and 3. This guide provides more detailed guidance around steps 2 and 3 of the inventory process. Available as a Google Doc or PDF.

Open Data Licensing Standard

  • Licensing Open Data: Resources and Practices (gdoc). Research conducted by our intern on data licensing options available and considerations for deployment in San Francisco.
  • Licenses - Inventory (gsheet). Inventory of available licenses we reviewed and licensing policies across cities and states.
  • Licensing Open Data: A practical guide (PDF). Our favorite resource on licensing we uncovered during our research.
